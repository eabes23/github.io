About PQC

Penn Quantum Computing Club is a student organization that aims to be your primary quantum computing resource for undergraduates at Penn. We provide resources for students who are just beginning to learn about quantum computing to those who are looking for internships and jobs in the quantum computing space. Throughout the year, Penn Quantum Computing will host events that expose students to new ideas in the field of quantum computing, including panels and educational workshops, office visits, senior speakers, social events, and networking opportunities.
